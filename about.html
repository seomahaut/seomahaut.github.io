Bonjour & 안녕하세요!

My name is Mahaut Seo, and I am an international exchange student from France currently studying at the beautiful Konkuk University in Seoul. Since arriving in South Korea, I have been completely captivated by the country's incredible energy, deep-rooted traditions, and warm hospitality.

This travel portal is a university project designed to act as both a visual diary and a practical resource. It aggregates my personal travel logs, food tasting notes, and public transport survival tricks for other international students and tourists looking to explore the beautiful Korean peninsula.
