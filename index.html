Welcome to Korea

A Harmonious Blend of Ancient Heritage and Futurism

South Korea is a destination where the 5,000-year-old dynasty history stands side-by-side with futuristic sky-high architectures and digital innovations. Whether you seek the peaceful serenity of mountain temples or the neon-lit late-night energy of urban Seoul, Korea offers a sensory adventure like no other.

This travel website is designed as a student project to share the rich landscape, delicious local dishes, and practical navigation tips I've gathered while living as an exchange student at Konkuk University.
